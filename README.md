## One-line Description

**Greeny Store** is an e-commerce platform based in Dublin, Ireland, offering fresh organic fruits, smoothies, cold-pressed juices, and fruit bowls. It enables convenient access to healthy, locally-sourced, and organic products with simple online ordering and prompt delivery.

## About

Greeny Store addresses the rising demand in Ireland for convenient, organic food choices. Our target market includes health-conscious individuals, families, and people with special dietary needs (e.g., diabetic-friendly products). The website delivers a seamless shopping experience for fresh organic produce and related goods, with a focus on fast home delivery and easy online ordering.

---

## Features

- Browse and search for fresh organic fruits, juices, smoothies, and fruit bowls
- Detailed product descriptions and high-quality images
- Add items to cart and place online orders
- Responsive, accessibility-focused design for all devices
- Product categories including diabetic-friendly and locally-sourced options
- Fast delivery within Dublin area
- Shopping cart with local storage persistence
- Product modals with quantity selectors and special requests
- Bundle deals for smoothies, juices, and detox drinks
- Form validation with real-time feedback
- FAQ accordion with smooth animations

---

## Market Opportunity

The organic food market in Ireland is expanding as consumers become more conscious of healthy lifestyles. Greeny Store meets this growing demand by providing:
- Fresh, pesticide-free, locally-sourced organic produce
- Variety of ready-to-eat options (fruit bowls, juices, smoothies)
- Solutions for dietary needs and preferences

## Design Process

### Color Palette

- **Primary Green:** `#8bbf41` (organic, fresh, natural)
- **Dark Green:** `#12372A` (trust, stability)
- **Accent Lime:** `#a2c108` (energy, vitality)
- **Cream Background:** `#F9F7F3` (clean, minimal)

### Typography

- **Headings:** Playfair Display (elegant, professional)
- **Body Text:** Lato (clean, readable)

### Design Decisions

- Green color scheme reinforces organic/natural brand identity
- Large hero images showcase product freshness and quality
- Card-based layout for products ensures easy browsing
- Sticky navigation for seamless user experience
- Clear call-to-action buttons guide users through purchase journey

## Getting Started

To view and run the site locally:
1. Clone this repository:
   ```
   git clone https://github.com/binayg24/friendlyfruitsproject.git
   ```
2. Open `index.html` in your web browser.
3. For custom deployments or features, review the structure and modify files as required.

*No additional dependencies required for static HTML/CSS/JS view.*
